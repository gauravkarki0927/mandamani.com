Sayeri

Khayaalo main rehetii ho yesa lagta hai jaise paas ho tum
Nido mai aati ho to lagta hai koi khaas ho tum
In saaso ko kyaa maalum kii kitna pyaar hai hame tumse
Chupa k raktaa hai ye dil tume dhadkano k aalfaz ho tum

Muskile haar jaati hai tume dekhne par chaand vii sarmaa jaaye
Kavi na vulaa paane wala dilkaa ek ahesas ho tum
Ek tarfa pyaar hai dill chahata hai kii ishaar karu
Dar lagta hai kahi khoo naa du dill kii saas ho tum

Vulakar vii naa mita sake pyaar kii itihaas ho tum
Sachhaaiyo kii khusbu hai tumme jaise 2 palo kii bishwas ho tum
Jindagi ab chahane lagi hai jina chatii hai tumhaare sang
Sarma se jhuk jaye duniya meri mahobat kii mithas ho tum

Dekhtii ho inn aakho mai par najar milatii nahi ho tum
Rehne do inn ghaaoko tute dill silaati nahi ho tum
Kohi aur vii hoga tumhaare pixe iss liye paresaan dekhtaa hu tumhe
Kehe do hume chupake rakhenge par isjajat dilaati nahi ho tum
Mandamani
